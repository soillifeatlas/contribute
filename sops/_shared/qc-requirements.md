# QC requirements

Applies to T2 submissions (where the contributing lab runs the extraction). For T1 contributions, CeMESS handles all QC at its end.

## Internal standard

- **EquiSPLASH (Avanti)** is the reference IS. Alternatives (SPLASH LIPIDOMIX or custom deuterated mixes) accepted with documentation.
- Spike before extraction (pre-extraction) recommended so the IS tracks extraction efficiency.
- Lot number and spike amount must be recorded on submission.

## Blanks

- Include at least one extraction blank per batch (solvents only, no biomass).
- Blanks are shipped alongside samples.

## Replication

- Three technical replicates per species is ideal where biomass permits.
- Single replicate accepted for rare/low-biomass organisms with a note explaining the limitation.

## Contamination

- Use glassware baked at 500 °C for 5 h before use.
- Use LC-MS-grade solvents.
- Wash disposable plastic (caps, pipette tips) with deionised water + ethanol + acetone.
