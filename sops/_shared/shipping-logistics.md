## Shipping formats by tier

| Tier | Typical format | Cold chain | Lead time |
|---|---|---|---|
| T1 biomass (freeze-dried) | Sealed screw-cap tubes | None — ambient | 2–5 days EU; 7–14 global |
| T1 biomass (frozen) | Dry ice, polystyrene box | Required; ≥2 kg dry ice for 48 h | Overnight within EU; express global |
| T1 biomass (live) | Agar slants / broth in sealed tubes | Depends on taxon | Overnight required |
| T2 lipid extract | Screw-cap amber glass, N₂-purged, −20 °C | Dry ice required | Overnight required |

## Customs

- **Within EU:** no customs declaration for non-commercial biological samples between academic institutions.
- **Outside EU:** attach a pro-forma invoice marked "non-commercial research samples, no commercial value" and HS code 3002.10.00.00 (biological material for research).
- **CITES-regulated species:** contact us before shipping. We handle the import permit on our side.

## Biosafety

- We accept BSL-1 and BSL-2 materials. BSL-3 requires prior written agreement.
- Pathogen status must be declared on the submission form and labelled on the outer packaging.
